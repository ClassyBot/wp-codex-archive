On some Thursdays at 20:30 UTC we have an IRC "meetup" where everyone gets together in the #wordpress-dev IRC channel to chat about the current release cycle.

Agenda and meeting summaries are posted on wpdevel. Whilst meetings are in progress, please help to stick to the agenda items by refraining from non-agenda chat until the meetings have completed.

Chat logs showing previous meets can be seen here.

You can find information about the WordPress irc channels here.
