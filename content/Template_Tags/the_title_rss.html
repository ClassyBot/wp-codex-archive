Description[edit]

Displays the title of the current post, formatted for RSS. This tag must be within The Loop.

Usage[edit]

 <?php the_title_rss(); ?> 

Example[edit]

Displays the post title in an RSS title tag. 

<item>
    <title><?php the_title_rss(); ?></title>

Parameters[edit]

This tag has no parameters.
