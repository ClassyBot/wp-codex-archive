WordPress Version 2.0.6 consists mainly of minor bugfixes, minor feature enhancement, and security fixes (multiple XSS issues and a potential SQL injection issue). .

There is a known bug related to Feedburner, but a workaround is available in the form of a fix to wp-includes/functions.php.
